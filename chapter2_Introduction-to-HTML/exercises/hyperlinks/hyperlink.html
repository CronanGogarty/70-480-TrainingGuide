mailto links

Using the mailto protocol to send emails from hyperlinks;
simple mailto: name@domain.com
mailto using name and email address; "Firstname Lastname<firstnamelastname@domain.com>": firstnamelastname@domain.com
mailto with a comma-seperated list of recipients; "user1@domain.com,user2@domain.com": list@domain.com
mailto with a CC; "user@domain.com?cc=carboncopy@domain.com": carboncopy@domain.com
mailto with a BCC; "user@domain.com?bcc=blindcopy@domain.com": blindcopy@domain.com
mailto with a CC and BCC; "user@domain.com?cc=copy@domain.com&bcc=blindcopy@domain.com": cc-and-bcc@domain.com
mailto with a message body; "user@domain.com?body=Important message body!": message@domain.com
mailto with a multiline message; "user@domain.com?body=Multiline%0Amessage": multilinemessage@domain.com
mailto with a subject and a message; "user@domain.com?subject=Important Subject&body=this is very important": subject-and-message@domain.com
